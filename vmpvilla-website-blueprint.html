Step 4 — Notifications

How to Keep Customers Notified

Multi-channel approach so guests never miss a deal, reminder, or update.

🔔
Browser Push Notifications
Via Firebase Cloud Messaging (free). Guest opts in on first visit. Send: booking confirmations, flash deals ("This weekend — 20% off!"), seasonal alerts.
📧
Email Automation
Brevo (free up to 300/day). Triggered emails: booking confirm, pre-arrival checklist, "You're arriving tomorrow", post-stay review request, loyalty offer.
📱
WhatsApp Broadcast
WhatsApp Business API (via AiSensy or Interakt). Send seasonal offers, festival greetings, last-minute deals to opted-in past guests. ₹0.58/message.
💬
SMS Alerts
Via Textlocal or MSG91. Booking confirmations, OTP for login, check-in day reminder. SMS reaches guests with no data/WhatsApp.
📲
PWA App Notifications
Once guest installs your PWA, you can push notifications to their homescreen just like a native app — zero app store needed.
📸
Instagram & Google Posts
Schedule posts via Meta Business Suite. Google Business Profile posts appear in Maps search results — free organic reach for offers.

Booking data in Supabase. Blog/content in Sanity — you control updates.

UPI, cards, net banking. Easy Indian integration.
Notifications

Push notifications (Firebase) + email automation (Brevo, free tier).
